RGB smoothie

Description

A healthy and filling smoothie. Perfect for the macro concientious.

Ingrediants

  • Spinach
  • Frozen strawberries
  • Frozen blueberries
  • Water
  • Peanut butter powder
  • Chocolate protein powder

Optional Ingrediants

  • Kiwi

Adding a kiwi to this smoothie makes it a great source of many neccesary vitamins.

Preperation

  1. Add spinach, strawberries, and blueberries to the cup of a blender
  2. Add water till level with top of ingrediants
  3. Blend until mostly smooth
  4. Add peanut butter powder and protein powder
  5. Blend until homogenous

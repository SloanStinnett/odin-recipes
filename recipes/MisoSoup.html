Miso Soup

Description

A warming and simple dish perfect for cold, dreary weather.

Ingrediants

  • Miso paste
  • Dashi flakes
  • Firm tofu
  • Scallions
  • Water

Optional Ingrediants

  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms
  • Sea weed

I enjoy adding the ingrediants above to make the soup a more filling meal.

Preperation

  1. Bring water to a boil in a large sauce pan.
  2. Add miso paste and dashi flakes
  3. Add the tofu
  4. Add scallions and wait for soup to cool
